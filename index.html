Make + Learn = Fun is a place for makers of all ages to make and learn and have fun. We provide project resources, educational videos, and informative podcasts to help young makers explore the world of science, technology, and art through hands on learning.


I am a software developer by trade, computer and electrical engineer by schooling, and most importantly a father. I want to create a place to share my love of learning through making with the world.
